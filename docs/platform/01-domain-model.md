# Access Management Platform - Domain Model

> Version: 1.0.0
> Last Updated: July 2026

## Core Entities

### Lead
A prospective customer who has expressed interest in the platform.

### Application
A formal request to join the platform.

### Customer
An approved commercial entity with a billing relationship.

### Partner
A third-party company that manages multiple customers.

### Organization (Tenant)
A fully isolated workspace within the platform.

### Site
A physical location belonging to an organization.

### Access Point
A physical or logical entry/exit point.

### Credential
A method of identity verification.

### User
A person with access to the platform.

### Policy
Rules governing access decisions.

### Session
An active access session.

### AuditLog
A record of all actions in the platform.
