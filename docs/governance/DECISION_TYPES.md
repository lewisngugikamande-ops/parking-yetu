# Decision Types

## Accepted

Normal architectural decision.

---

## Accepted (Backfilled)

Decision implemented before documentation.

Evidence must reference:

- commits
- code
- documentation
- audit findings

---

## Superseded

Replaced by another ADR.

---

## Deprecated

No longer recommended.

---

## Rejected

Considered but not adopted.

---

## Proposed

Awaiting approval.
