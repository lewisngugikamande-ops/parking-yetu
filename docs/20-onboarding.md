# Parking Yetu - Onboarding Guide

## Overview

This guide walks you through setting up Parking Yetu for the first time. Complete all steps to go from zero to live parking operations.

**Estimated time:** 1-2 hours

---

## Step 1: Create Organization

1. **Register** for a Parking Yetu account
2. **Create your organization** (e.g., "St. Mary's Church")
3. **Select your plan** (or start free trial)

**Result:** Organization created

---

## Step 2: Add Locations

1. Navigate to **Admin → Locations**
2. Click **Add Location**
3. Enter details:
   - Name (e.g., "Main Campus")
   - Address
   - Capacity
   - Operating hours
4. Click **Save**

**Result:** Location created

---

## Step 3: Add Gates

1. Navigate to **Admin → Gates**
2. Click **Add Gate**
3. Enter details:
   - Name (e.g., "Gate A - Main")
   - Type (Entry, Exit, or Both)
   - Location
4. Click **Save**

**Result:** Gates configured

---

## Step 4: Configure Parking Rules

1. Navigate to **Admin → Parking Rules**
2. Set:
   - **Time Limit** (e.g., 180 minutes)
   - **Alert Threshold** (e.g., 120 minutes)
3. Click **Save**

**Result:** Parking rules configured

---

## Step 5: Add Security Personnel

1. Navigate to **Admin → Users**
2. Click **Create User**
3. Enter:
   - Email
   - Password
   - Name
   - Role (Guard or Security Manager)
   - Location
   - Gate
   - Shift
4. Click **Create**

**Result:** Security personnel added

---

## Step 6: Add VIP Vehicles

1. Navigate to **Admin → VIP Management**
2. Enter license plate
3. Click **Add VIP**

**Result:** VIP vehicles registered

---

## Step 7: Generate Entrance QR

1. Navigate to **Admin → Entrance QR**
2. Click **Generate** (or download existing)
3. **Print** the QR code
4. **Place** at the entrance gate

**Result:** Entrance QR ready for drivers

---

## Step 8: Test Entry Flow

1. **Open** the Entry page
2. Enter:
   - Plate: `TEST123`
   - Driver: `Test Driver`
   - Phone: `0712345678`
3. Click **Check In**
4. **Verify** QR code appears

**Result:** Entry flow working

---

## Step 9: Test Exit Flow

1. **Log in** as a security guard
2. **Scan** the QR code from Step 8
3. **Verify** vehicle details
4. Click **Release**
5. **Confirm** exit

**Result:** Exit flow working

---

### For Administrators

1. How to check in

---

## Step 11: Go Live

1. **Communicate** to all drivers
2. **Switch** from manual to digital
3. **Monitor** first day closely
4. **Address** issues immediately

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: Can't log in
- Check email and password
- Reset password if needed
- Contact support

### Issue: QR not scanning
- Check camera permissions
- Ensure QR is clear
- Try manual plate entry

### Issue: Vehicle not found
- Check spelling of plate
- Ensure vehicle is parked
- Try scanning QR

### Issue: Exit not working
- Check guard permissions
- Verify vehicle status
- Try admin override
