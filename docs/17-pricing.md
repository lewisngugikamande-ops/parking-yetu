# Parking Yetu - Pricing

## Overview

Parking Yetu offers flexible pricing plans designed for organizations of all sizes. Each plan includes core parking operations features with additional capabilities unlocked at higher tiers.

---

## Plans

### Free Trial
**Duration:** 14 days
**Price:** $0

**Includes:**
- Single location
- 5 users
- 100 parking sessions
- QR entry/exit
- Basic dashboard
- Email support

**Best for:** Testing the platform

---

### Starter
**Price:** $49/month
**Annual:** $470/year (save 20%)

**Includes:**
- 1 location
- 10 users
- 500 parking sessions/month
- QR entry/exit
- Security dashboard
- Admin dashboard
- Audit logs
- Email support

**Best for:** Small churches, schools, businesses

---

### Business
**Price:** $99/month
**Annual:** $950/year (save 20%)

**Includes:**
- Up to 3 locations
- 25 users
- 2,000 parking sessions/month
- All Starter features
- VIP & Staff management
- Gate control
- Reporting & analytics
- Priority support

**Best for:** Growing organizations with multiple locations

---

### Enterprise
**Price:** Custom
**Annual:** Custom

**Includes:**
- Unlimited locations
- Unlimited users
- Unlimited parking sessions
- All Business features
- Custom branding
- Advanced reporting
- API access
- Dedicated support
- SLAs

**Best for:** Large organizations with complex needs
