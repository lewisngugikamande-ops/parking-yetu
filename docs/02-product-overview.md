# Parking Yetu - Product Overview

## What Is Parking Yetu?
An enterprise parking operations platform enabling organizations to manage parking across multiple locations.

## Key Capabilities
- Vehicle entry and exit management
- QR code-based access control
- Security personnel management
- Real-time operational dashboards
- Comprehensive audit logging
- Multi-location support
- VIP and staff vehicle management
- Operational reporting and analytics
