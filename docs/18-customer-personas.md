# Parking Yetu - Customer Personas

## Primary Personas

### 1. Church Administrator

**Role:** Operations Manager / Facilities Director

**Organization:** Large church with 500+ attendees

**Pain Points:**
- Sunday morning parking chaos
- VIPs and staff don't get priority
- No visibility into parking issues
- Manual tracking is unreliable
- Security incidents are hard to investigate

**Needs:**
- Smooth parking flow during peak times
- VIP and staff prioritization
- Real-time visibility into parking
- Automated tracking and reporting
- Incident investigation capability

**Success Metrics:**
- Sunday morning congestion reduced by 50%
- No VIP complaints about parking
- Incident resolution time under 24 hours

---

### 2. Security Manager

**Role:** Head of Security

**Organization:** Multi-site business campus

**Pain Points:**
- Inconsistent guard procedures
- No shift accountability
- Hard to track vehicle movement
- Manual logs are incomplete
- Difficult to train new guards

**Needs:**
- Standardized exit procedures
- Shift logging and accountability
- Full vehicle tracking
- Digital logs that can't be lost
- Simple, intuitive interface

**Success Metrics:**
- 100% of exits logged
- Shift completion tracking
- 50% reduction in training time

---

### 3. Security Guard

**Role:** Security Personnel

**Organization:** Any organization with parking

**Pain Points:**
- Manual logbooks are tedious
- Hard to verify vehicle identity
- No clear procedures for exceptions
- Difficult to remember all vehicles
- Stress during peak times

**Needs:**
- Quick and easy exit processing
- QR verification
- Clear action guidance
- Simple interface
- Fast response during busy periods

**Success Metrics:**
- Exit processed in <30 seconds
- No vehicles released incorrectly
- Stress-free peak hours

---

### 4. Receptionist

**Role:** Front Desk / Visitor Management

**Organization:** Business or school

**Pain Points:**

- Hard to enforce parking rules
- Difficult to find vehicles

**Needs:**
- Simple check-in process
- Digital record keeping
- QR verification
- Rule enforcement
- Easy vehicle lookup

**Success Metrics:**
- Visitor check-in under 2 minutes
- 100% digital records
- No lost parking records

### 5. Driver
